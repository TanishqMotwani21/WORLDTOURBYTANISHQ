The Seven Wonders of the World are amazing monuments that attract millions of visitors every year.

1. Taj Mahal
Location : India
Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of Mumtaz Mahal.

2. Great Wall of China
Location : China
One of the longest structures ever built by humans.

3. Petra
Location : Jordan
An ancient city carved into pink-colored rock.

4. Christ the Redeemer
Location : Brazil
A giant statue of Jesus Christ overlooking Rio de Janeiro.

5. Machu Picchu
Location : Peru
An ancient Inca city located high in the Andes Mountains.

6. Chichen Itza
Location : Mexico
A famous Mayan archaeological site.

7. Colosseum
Location : Italy
A massive amphitheater built during the Roman Empire.
